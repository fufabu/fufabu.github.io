# Privacy Policy for Companion for J-6

**Last Updated: December 2, 2025**

## Introduction

Companion for J-6 ("we", "our", or "the App") is a MIDI controller application designed for use with the Roland J-6 Chord Synthesizer. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **App Settings**: MIDI channel configurations, BPM settings, and theme preferences that you configure within the app.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Crash Reports**: If the app crashes, we may collect diagnostic information to help us fix bugs and improve stability.
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics to understand how the app is used and to improve features.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Contact information
- Audio recordings or musical content
- MIDI data or musical performances

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Save your app preferences locally on your device
- Improve app stability and performance
- Understand usage patterns to enhance features

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

All app settings (MIDI channels, BPM, theme preferences) are stored **locally on your device** using SharedPreferences. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any server
- Can be deleted at any time through the app's settings

### Analytics and Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics to:

- Monitor app stability
- Collect anonymous usage statistics

This data is processed by Google Firebase in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

| Service              | Purpose         | Privacy Policy                                                  |
| -------------------- | --------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Firebase Crashlytics | Crash reporting | [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| Firebase Analytics   | Usage analytics | [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |

## MIDI Communication

The App communicates with MIDI devices (such as the Roland J-6) via USB MIDI connection.

This communication occurs **entirely on your device**. No MIDI data is transmitted to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Data Retention

- **Local Settings**: Retained on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app
- **Crash Reports**: Retained by Firebase for 90 days
- **Analytics Data**: Retained by Firebase according to their data retention policies

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View your settings within the app
- **Delete**: Clear all stored data via Settings > Data Management > Delete Saved Data
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics in your device settings

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Displaying a notice in the app (for significant changes)
